Getting Started

To get started, join an event you see on the home page! If you can't find one you're interested in, create your own event!

How can I see the events I joined?

Click on the dropdown menu and click on "My Events" to view events you have already joined! Get connected with other participants also!

How to create an event?

On the top navigation, click on create an event.

Want to see the profile of people who have joined a certain event?

To see the profile, go to the Event and under "List of Attendees" you'll be able to click on the users who have joined and see their profile.

What kind of events can I host?

It's mainly up to you! Host an event that is interesting to you!

Are you a company interested in hosting an event?

Add your website in the description so people can read more about it!
